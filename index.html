About the Game

Mobile Legends: Bang-Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. It is developed and published by Moonton. The game was released in 2016 and quickly became one of the most popular mobile games worldwide.

The game features two opposing teams of five players who compete to destroy the base of the opposing team while defending their own base. Players control characters known as "heroes," each with unique abilities and roles. The game is known for its strategic gameplay, frequent updates, and a large esports scene.
